Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 30, 2026

Klyr (the "App") is published by Jaggaro ("we", "us"). Klyr is an offline-first, privacy-first personal and small-business finance app — money tracking, accounts, people & udhaar (lending), budgets, reminders, and a business workspace for billing, customers, inventory, memberships, appointments, staff and rentals. This policy explains what data the App handles and how.

All of your financial data stays on your device. Klyr has no account, no sign-up, and no login. We do not operate any backend server or cloud database, and we do not sync, upload, or copy your data to Jaggaro or to any third party. Your records live only on the device where you installed the App, and work fully offline.

1. What the App stores on your device

Klyr saves the information you enter so the App is useful — all of it in the operating system's standard private app storage on your device:

  • Money records — transactions, accounts and balances, categories, budgets, recurring entries, and reminders.
  • People & debts — the people you add and the amounts you lend or borrow, settlements, and split-expense groups.
  • Business records (only if you use Business mode) — your business profile, customers and their khata (credit) ledger, products & services, bills/invoices, memberships, appointments, staff and salary, and rentals.
  • Attachments you choose to add (for example a photo of a receipt) — stored as a local file reference on your device.
  • Preferences — language, theme (light/dark), currency, personal/business workspace, and feature settings.
  • An optional app-lock PIN, kept in the device's secure keystore (see Section 4).

This data never leaves your device on its own. You can delete individual records inside the App, wipe everything from your device settings, or remove it all by uninstalling the App.

2. Backups, exports & sharing — only when you choose

Klyr can create files at your request, and you decide where they go:

  • Backups — you can export a backup file of your data and restore it later. The file is saved/shared only through your device's own share sheet, to a destination you pick.
  • Reports & statements — you can generate PDF or CSV reports and bills and share them (for example over WhatsApp or email).
  • Reminders / WhatsApp messages — when you tap to remind a customer, the App opens your messaging app with a pre-filled message; you choose whether to send it.

These files are handled entirely by you and your device's apps. Jaggaro never receives them. Once you share a file with another app or service, that app's or service's own privacy policy applies (for example WhatsApp).

3. The Klyr AI assistant is on-device

Klyr's built-in money assistant answers questions about your own numbers (for example "how much did I spend this month"). It runs entirely on your device using your local data — it is not a cloud AI, sends nothing to any server, and works offline.

4. App lock & biometrics

You can optionally protect the App with a PIN and/or your device biometric (fingerprint or face). The PIN is stored in the device's secure keystore. Biometric verification is performed by your device's operating system — Klyr only receives a yes/no result and never accesses or stores your fingerprint or face data.

5. What we do NOT collect ourselves

  • No name, email, phone number, address, or other contact info about you.
  • No account or login — the App has no sign-up.
  • No precise location accessed by the App itself.
  • We never upload, copy, or transmit your financial records, attachments, backups, or reports — they stay on-device.
  • No analytics or crash events sent to a Jaggaro-operated server (we do not operate any servers).

6. Third-party services — Google AdMob

Klyr is free and uses Google AdMob, a Google advertising service, to display advertisements (a banner, occasional full-screen interstitials at natural breaks, and an optional rewarded ad you can choose to watch to turn ads off for a short while). AdMob is the only third-party advertising SDK the App links. Klyr requests non-personalized ads only — it does not use your data for ad targeting. To serve and measure ads, AdMob may still collect or process the following device-level signals:

  • Advertising identifier — Android Advertising ID (AAID), depending on your device and your OS-level ad settings.
  • IP address — used by Google to infer approximate region for region-appropriate, non-personalized ads and fraud prevention.
  • Device model, OS version, screen size — for correctly rendering ads.
  • Ad-interaction events (impression, click, rewarded-ad completion) — for ad measurement and reporting.

Google's policies and data practices govern that processing. See Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy and AdMob's developer privacy notice at developers.google.com/admob/android/privacy. You can reset or limit the advertising identifier from your device Settings → Google → Ads. No other third-party SDK sends data off your device — no Jaggaro analytics, no crash-reporting server, no remote configuration, and no other ad networks. Ad serving is the only networked feature; everything else in Klyr works offline.

7. Permissions the App uses on Android

  • Internet & network state — used by Google AdMob to fetch and display ads, and to detect whether you are online. Not used for any Jaggaro analytics, tracking, or to upload your data.
  • Notifications — to show the bill, EMI, rent and other reminders you set. All reminders are scheduled locally on your device.
  • Biometric / fingerprint — only for the optional app lock (Section 4).
  • Photos / storage — to attach a receipt image to a record if you choose, and to save backups and PDF/CSV exports you create. The App accesses only the items you pick.
  • Camera — only if you choose to capture a photo to attach to a record.
  • Advertising ID & vibration — the advertising ID is used by AdMob (Section 6); vibration powers optional haptic feedback.

Some ad formats served by the Google AdMob SDK (for example video or rewarded ads) may use additional standard device capabilities to play the ad; these are controlled by Google's SDK, not by Jaggaro.

8. Data retention & security

Because your data is stored only on your device, you control its lifetime: it remains until you delete it in the App or uninstall the App. We recommend keeping a backup if your data matters to you, since uninstalling removes it permanently and we hold no copy to restore from.

9. Children's privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Because no personal data is collected by Jaggaro at all, COPPA and similar regimes do not apply to data we hold (none). Ads are served by Google AdMob under Google's policies.

10. Your choices

  • Grant or deny camera, photo, and notification access from your device Settings; features that don't need a given permission keep working.
  • Delete individual records inside the App at any time.
  • Export a backup before changing devices or uninstalling.
  • Reset or limit the advertising identifier from your device settings.
  • Remove all on-device data by uninstalling the App.

11. Changes to this policy

If material changes are made — for example, if a new third-party service is added in a future release — this page will be updated and the new Effective date will be displayed at the top.
