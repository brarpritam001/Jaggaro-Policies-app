Bubble Rush Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

1. Introduction

Bubble Rush (the "Game", "we", "us", or "our") is a free, single-player bubble-shooter arcade game developed and published by Jaggaro. This Privacy Policy explains what information is — and is not — involved when you play Bubble Rush on your mobile device.

Bubble Rush is designed to be privacy-friendly and offline-first. The Game does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal details to play. By installing and using the Game, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Jaggaro does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information about you. The Game has no login, no account system, and no Jaggaro-operated server or database. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or postal address.
  • Precise location data accessed by the Game itself.
  • Your photos, files, contacts, calendar, or other personal content.
  • Analytics or usage events sent to any server operated by Jaggaro.

The only data the Game works with is your local gameplay progress and settings, which stay on your device (see Section 3), and limited advertising-related signals processed by Google AdMob (see Section 4).

3. Local Storage & Offline Gameplay

Bubble Rush is fully playable offline. An internet connection is only used to load advertisements. All of your game data is saved locally on your device using the operating system's standard on-device storage, including:

  • Campaign progress — unlocked levels, stars earned, and best scores.
  • Soft-currency coins, daily-reward streaks, and login-streak state.
  • Lives, chest and reward-road progress, and unlocked achievements.
  • Preferences such as theme, music, sound effects, and haptics.

This information never leaves your device, is not visible to Jaggaro, and is not synced to any cloud. You can erase it at any time by using the in-game "Reset Progress" option or by uninstalling the Game.

4. Advertising (Google AdMob)

Bubble Rush is free and supported by advertising delivered through Google AdMob, a Google advertising service. To serve and measure ads, the Google Mobile Ads SDK may collect or process certain device-related identifiers and similar ad-serving information, which may include:

  • A device advertising identifier — the Android Advertising ID (AAID) or Apple's Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA), subject to your device's ad-tracking settings.
  • Your IP address, used by Google to infer an approximate region for relevant ads.
  • Coarse device information such as model, operating-system version, and screen size, used to render ads correctly.
  • Ad-interaction events (impressions, clicks, and rewarded-ad completions) used for ad delivery and measurement.

This processing is performed by Google under its own policies, not by Jaggaro. For details, see Google's Privacy Policy and the AdMob developer privacy information. You can reset or limit your advertising identifier and control personalized ads from your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads.
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

If you opt out of personalized ads, you may still see non-personalized ads.

5. Third-Party Services

The only third-party service integrated into Bubble Rush is Google AdMob, used solely to display advertisements as described above. The Game does not include any other analytics, crash-reporting, social-media, remote-configuration, or additional ad-network SDKs. We do not sell or share your personal data — and because we collect none, there is no personal data for us to sell or share.

6. Children's Privacy

Bubble Rush is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Advertising is configured to align with Google Play's and AdMob's policies. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us using the details below and we will address it promptly.

7. Data Security

Because Bubble Rush stores data only on your device and Jaggaro operates no servers, there is no server-side personal data for us to secure. Your local game data is protected by your device's own operating-system security. Any data handled for advertising is secured by Google in accordance with its policies.

8. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time — for example, to reflect new features or changes in advertising or legal requirements. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes will be reflected here and, where appropriate, in the Game's store listing. Continued use of the Game after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
